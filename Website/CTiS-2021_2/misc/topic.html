We are topic is Accurate Delivery of Online Advertising Based on Big Data Technology. With the rapid development of global online advertising industry, big data emerged as an auxiliary analysis technology. It is a kind of technology, big data calculation advertising push users, it is based on advertising requirements, comprehensive consideration of product and user characteristics. Select the most matching goods from the advertising library and push them to target customers by guessing user characteristics. Thus increase the advertising push strategy and increase the conversion rate of goods. With the development of international trade, more and more people choose to shop on the Internet, so the advertising push of the shopping platform is particularly important. It will help users quickly find items of interest. From the computing aspect of big data, it can assist the platform to screen and match target customer groups. So as to achieve accurate advertising and increase the rate of commodity purchase. Big data technology helps in the accurate placement of online advertising and the evaluation of the effectiveness of advertising.

Ge, T., & Wu, X. (2021). Accurate Delivery of Online Advertising and the Evaluation of Advertising Effect Based on Big Data Technology. Mobile Information Systems, 2021.
