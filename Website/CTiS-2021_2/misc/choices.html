Technology Choices

The page or area describing the choices available to us through or by your chosen technology/topic.

With the advent of the information age and the continuous progress of computer science, big data has gradually become an indispensable tool in modern life.  The emergence of big data as a new model provides unparalleled composition and value for enterprises.  The growth of big data has brought challenges to the fields of processing, analysis, sharing and visualization.  This brings us new choices, opportunities and challenges.   The strong point of big data computing is that it can use computers to analyze massive data, and then get more valuable information, and this information obtained through a large number of calculations is the real value of big data. On top of pure profit-making, big data has a more comprehensive use, not to increase income as the main function.  Big data is helpful for understanding the spread of diseases, planning disaster relief measures after natural disasters, and formulating detailed policy plans.  Big data can also unlock the secrets of our current world and help us plan the future path.
Big data technology can bring many new possibilities to people ' s lives because of its advanced and scientific characteristics. How to use this technology is an urgent need for us to discuss.  Big data technology can efficiently process fuzzy information, intelligently process network information data, and greatly reduce the cost of data processing.  With reasonable utilization, it well serves many fields in our modern life.  However, big data itself is also a double-edged sword.  It will bring us potential risks while providing us with convenience, which requires us or enterprises to weigh and choose between the two.
For example, ecommerce companies such as Alibaba and Amazon can judge a user 's preferences, personality and value orientation by analyzing big data, and draw a user 's ' user portrait ', which helps organizations identify the target group they should target, and then accurately deliver advertising goods to target users to enhance the success rate of commodity sales.  At the same time, these enterprises can also choose to sell this precise user portrait as a specific product or service to other enterprises or departments to obtain more benefits.  However, it is undeniable that the user ' s personal information as a commodity sales of this commercial behavior is very high risk and unreasonable, which will lead to the leakage of personal privacy of users and the deterioration of the Internet ecological environment, and ultimately will cause adverse effects on the production and operation of the enterprise itself.  Therefore, how to use big data this tool is very important, enterprises and individuals need to make a good choice, reasonable and safe use of big data this tool, Big data has the ability to fundamentally change the business model. Whether through drastic changes or smooth transition, how to use this tool properly and effectively is particularly important.
In addition to enterprises, big data computing can also play a greater role in government departments.  In terms of political decision-making, the government can understand people ’ s opinions on a certain policy by collecting poll information in various regions, which helps the government to modify or formulate more effective policies that are in line with people ’ s expectations.  In addition, the government can also collect the opinions and suggestions of people who belong to different groups in different occupations in various regions, so as to judge the support rate of their own political parties in a certain type of population, which helps the political parties to understand their competitive advantages, and then develop a system with more distinctive characteristics of the party itself to attract voters.  However, as the previous example of e-commerce enterprises, big data calculation may also have a negative effect in government departments.  For example, political parties may use voter information from big data analysis to make targeted policies to attack hostile parties, which may lead to endless political struggles within the top government.  Therefore, how to use the tool of big data calculation well has become a crucial issue.
The era of big data needs the help of artificial intelligence to create more benefits for society.  The application of computer network technology can greatly improve the security and convenience of the network, have more efficient and accurate data processing, optimize computer technology, and have a great impact on people 's lives and work.
In summary, Big data because of its unique nature and powerful practical role, he needs users to make full preparations to seriously consider whether their choice can play its practical role well.  To avoid unnecessary trouble while profiting from its advantages.
