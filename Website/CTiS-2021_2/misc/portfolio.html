Project Portfolio


Our team divides project work into four phases including: Analysis(planning stage, choose topic, divide up the work), Design (early prototypes, summarising the proposal, start creating slides for the Project Proposal Presentation), Development (look at our topic in more depth,exploring the opportunities, risks, choices for the topic we investigate,making Project Presentation slides), and Implementation( designing the team pages,making team website more complete, final check for all workings).

Team members roles and responsibilities:


At the planning stage we assign our team roles,recorder duties include accurate indicating each meeting time date, member attendance.And focus on the record for later view, write down any details or ideas during meeting for further viewing, researcher duties include find relevant materials for the topic, select useful information from the materials supporting arguments.Add the information to presentation slides and website.Web developer responsible for making the web page, manage files on github and integrated landscaping pages.During the task while we focus on our assigned roles we might not only carried out our own responsibilities but also cooperated with the rest part.For example recorder and researcher can also do some web design work,web designer can do some of the research. Team members roles is the way by each team members will work towards the success of this assignment on each role varies to some extent as well.

For better cooperation during lockdown,we formed a Wechat discuss group to track discussions and publicise the assignment(we created a Individual team meeting time reocrd page named"meeting time" put it on github). There are few collaborative method approved by every team memebers help us approach to the challenges during lockdown. 1.Reflection: When one team member has mention a interesting view or point, another members should be a good ilstener understanding of the content teamates mentioned and then processes it. 2. Clarification: During meeting if anyone who confused about his work he should ask questions throughout the meeting, Make certain that every team members can understand now what we working on.If we cannot figure out the answer we might need to ask our TA in case.3. Summarization: Summarizing the main points of the received message in a clear way,And record all information we gathered for each meeting by the recorder.
