Technology Opportunities

The page or area describing the opportunities presented by your chosen technology/topic.

Big Data technology is the ability to quickly obtain valuable information from all types of data. Nowadays with the arrival of the big data era, the advancement of big data technology It provides a new direction for Online Advertising development. Contrasted with traditional advertising, easy and efficiency gather information from all types of data and less cost give big data advertising an edge. The rapid development of big data technology had a big influence on to the traditional advertising and it also with tremendous potential for digital advertising industry.(Ge & Wu, 2021)

One big advantage of big data is that it can help advertisers find target users and deliver personalized advertisements to them, once in a unite state an angry father called nearby supermarket to complain because they deliver a pregnant product advertisement to his daughter,but a week later, the father apologized to the supermarket, Why? Because his daughter is really pregnant. It turned out that through big data analysis. the supermarket found that a woman would buy a lot of fragrance free hand cream, multivitamins, and calcium tablets after pregnancy, and his daughter was buying these things.Thus, supermarkets can accurately predict the pregnancy of female customers and start deliver advertisements. Many social platform and search engine for example like Facebook and Google, they leverage big data create more appealing ads and deliver their ads in wider acceptance ways,based on the big data analytics, collect extensive quantity of personal data about what we're like, what we're share normally. Most of these commercials work helped users find the products or services they actually want could improve the quality of life. Digital advertisers can have insight into the original data, gave them a valuable insight into the problems of marketing decisions and strategies. Process structured and unstructured data, bears witness to the big data technology could help advertisers find them target users and also discover modern trends and patterns, improve them competitive edge Hence, many traditional advertising industry is now quickly turning they service to personalized and highly targeted digital advertising. Multi-dimensional according to the time, area and people, the advertisement can be accurately pushed to the target user browsing, accurate advertising can continuously spread the information to every corner of the Internet that everyone can see , which is more advanced than traditional advertising.Not only for online shopping and services, Big Data technology is also does an excellent job of Hyper-localized advertising. Hyper-localized advertising which means deliver the specific ads to the to the right person in the first place, nowadays because mobile devices has been popularized as never before, presented digital advertisers with an huge opportunity deliver mobile-specific ads to them target users. By analysis of the social data and location data, stores can send out ads offering discount coupons, gift certificates to the user's near them location. Hyper-localized advertising has proved to increase in customers and conversion rates. Nevertheless, some users might get uncomfortable with Location-based services,because they real-time location will be shared to advertisers. So when advertisers serve ads to users they should be conscious of users' privacy not steps over the line. (Philip, 2014)

Big data has three characteristics which are, Velocity,Volume, and Variety,First, velocity which means speed at big data created and collected,volume which means the volume of information, the variety which means whole scope of the data covered. Big data types can be divided into structured or unstructured. Big data usually collected from users comments from variety of social networks, Also big data could collected through voluntary way, such as online questionnaire survey.Big data stored in computer databases,output massive complex data will be analyze by the Specific software ( Segal, 2021).

Another point is for Price Optimization and improve service quality,big data analytics allows advertiser use their big data solutions to build models that not only show not consumers' personalized consumption habit and define much different types of customers will be willing to pay under different circumstances.Based on analytics also could help them improve their services, by analyze past data to predict the future. Therefore, enterprises can not only making current decisions, but also prepare for the future. Insight into the impact of different variables in the production process.This provides them with a competitive advantage and a more flexible framework for decision-making and risk management(Sriramoju, 2017).
